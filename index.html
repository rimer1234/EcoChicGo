EcoChic Go

The Outfit's Sustainability Score is: The Higg Materials Sustainability Index a sustainability tool for the apparel, footwear, and textiles industry. It Measures 5 Environmental Impacts of Material Production: Global warming, Nutrient pollution (eutrophication), Water scarcity, Fossil Fuel Depletion, Chemistry. Lower Higgs MSI scores are better.
